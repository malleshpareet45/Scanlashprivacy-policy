ScanLash LLC ("Scan Lash," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy ("Policy") explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our eyelash extension services ("Services") or access our platform (the "Platform") at scanlash.com (or any affiliated websites or mobile applications). By using the Services or Platform, you consent to the practices described in this Policy.

If you do not agree with our practices, please do not use our Services or Platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide, improve, and personalize our Services. The types of information include:

“The app does not store, retain, or share any face data. All face-related processing occurs locally on the user’s device.”

Personal Information

  • Contact Details: Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address provided during booking or registration.
  • Payment Information: Billing details (e.g., credit/debit card numbers) processed via secure third-party payment gateways (we do not store full card details).
  • Health and Preference Data: Allergies, sensitivities, medical conditions, lash preferences, and aftercare notes shared during intake or consultations (required for safe service delivery).
  • Appointment Details: Dates, times, locations, and service types booked.

Non-Personal Information

  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, time spent on the Platform, and referral sources (collected via cookies and analytics tools).
  • Location Data: Approximate location for scheduling mobile appointments or studio availability (with your consent).

Sensitive Information

We may collect health-related data (e.g., allergies or eye conditions) solely for service safety. This is treated with heightened care and not shared without explicit consent.

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent, in compliance with COPPA.

2. How We Collect Information

  • Directly from You: When you book appointments, create an account, submit forms, or contact support.
  • Automatically: Through cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies as you navigate the Platform. You can manage cookie preferences via your browser settings.
  • From Third Parties: Payment processors, analytics providers (e.g., Google Analytics), or service partners (e.g., SMS confirmation providers) with your consent.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Providing and managing Services (e.g., scheduling, reminders, and personalized recommendations).
  • Processing payments and preventing fraud.
  • Communicating with you (e.g., confirmations, updates, marketing emails with opt-out options).
  • Improving the Platform and Services (e.g., analyzing usage trends or training technicians anonymously).
  • Ensuring safety (e.g., reviewing health disclosures for allergy risks).
  • Legal compliance (e.g., retaining records for audits).

We may aggregate or de-identify information for analytics and improvements; aggregated data will not identify you personally.

4. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf (payment processors, hosting providers, analytics tools).
  • With Your Consent: When you ask us to share information with another party.
  • Legal Requests: If required by law or to protect our rights, safety, or property.

5. Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect information. However, no system is completely secure; if we learn of a breach that compromises sensitive information, we will notify affected users as required by law.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information as long as necessary to provide Services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. When data is no longer required, we securely delete or anonymize it.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below. We will respond in accordance with applicable law.

8. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate the Platform, remember preferences, and analyze usage. You can control cookies via your browser settings; disabling certain cookies may affect your experience.

9. International Transfers

If we transfer your information across borders, we will take steps required by law to protect it, such as using standard contractual clauses or other safeguards.

10. Children

Our Services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 without parental consent.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy. If changes are material, we will notify you by posting a prominent notice on the Platform or by other means.
